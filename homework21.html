I was fortunate to work with the Places for Project Teams product throughout an entire software lifecycle--from initial product conception through the actual product launch

I have a Ph.D. from the University of California, Irvine, where I specialization in Artificial Intelligence, and bachelors degrees in Computer Science and Electrical Engineeering.

At Irvine, I taught Introduction to Software Enginnering, a sophomore core class, and Software Processes, a graduate seminar.
